1st Self-training Self-Driving Challenge

The challenge website has been released before August 1 2021.

This challenge is a part of ICCV2021 workshop "Self-supervised Learning for Next-Generation Industry-level Autonomous Driving". The aim of this track is to utilize both labeled data and unlabled data to achieve industry-level autonomous driving solutions

For 2D object detection track, we provide a real-word training dataset with 10 million images of which 5K are labeled and 5K/10K validation/testing labeled images for evaluation. This dataset has been collected throughout diverse scenarios in cities in China and contains scenes in a wide variety of places, objects and weather conditions such as highways, city streets, country roads, raining weather, etc.

This challenge will be released before August 1st, 2021 to enable participants to evaluate their techniques. Challenge participants with the most successful and innovative entries will be invited to present at this workshop and will receive awards. A 10,000 USD cash prize will be awarded to the top performers in each task and 2nd and 3rd places will be awarded with 5,000 USD.
